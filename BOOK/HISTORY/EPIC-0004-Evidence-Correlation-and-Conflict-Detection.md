# EPIC-0004 — Evidence Correlation and Conflict Detection Foundation

EPIC-0004 added the first evidence correlation layer and conflict detector.

Nexiora can now distinguish between convergent evidence, contradictory evidence, neutral observations, and insufficient evidence. This prepares the laboratory for safer recommendations because the system can explicitly flag contested hypotheses before any human promotion decision.

Core rule preserved:

> Nexiora may recommend. Only a human may promote.
